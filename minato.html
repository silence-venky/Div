Minato Namikaze

Minato Namikaze is the Fourth Hokage of Konoha and the father of Naruto Uzumaki.

He is known for his incredible speed and is often referred to as the "Yellow Flash" due to his signature technique, the Flying Thunder God Jutsu. Minato played a crucial role in protecting the village during the Nine-Tails' attack and sacrificed his life to seal the beast within his newborn son, Naruto.

Minato is a highly skilled and intelligent ninja, respected by his peers and remembered for his bravery and selflessness. His legacy continues to influence the events of the Naruto series, as his son inherits his determination and spirit.

Minato's story is one of heroism, sacrifice, and the enduring impact of a father's love on his child. His contributions to the village and his family are celebrated throughout the series, making him a beloved character in the Naruto universe.
